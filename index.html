Desert sand (color)

Desert sand is a very light and very weakly saturated reddish yellow colour which corresponds specifically to the coloration of sand. It may also be regarded as a deep tone of beige.

Waterfalls

Waterfalls purify the water as they flow into various rivers. Purification of water provides oxygen to various aquatic life forms, allowing them to thrive. So, without waterfalls and their purification of water, we wouldn't have any fish to eat, and other aquatic animals would perish, causing the Ecosystem to suffer.
